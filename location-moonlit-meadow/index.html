Location: Moonlit Meadow

Kurama

I shared one of the base locations on the Discord server, so why not share it here as well!

Moonlit Meadow is a serene and grassy area that comes alive under the glow of the moon where shooting stars can often be seen. Cats can take a walk in the meadow, play games, stargaze and unwind. At the center of the meadow, cats can find a campfire and sit around it to tell nighttime stories.

What do you think? It's my personal favorite. :)
